Why We Do It

Unbound believes that a restorative approach to prisoners, former prisoners, and all those affected by crime and incarceration can make communities safer and healthier. By helping those who have commited mistakes in their lives to learn the proper skill set and become not only excelent and hirable workers but productive memebers of society. We prepare them for the future so they wont ever think about making the same mistakes again.

Why Help Prisoners?

Katie was released from prison three months ago, but everyone she meets disappears as soon as they find out about her past. Katie wonders, “Why won’t anyone give me a chance to prove I’m a hard working productive member of society?" This isn’t just a storie. Unbound meets people in these situations every day. Chances are you’ve met them too. The 700,000 men and women that our criminal justice system releases from prison each year return to communities around the country—including yours with no job expirience or job training. The numbers are staggering, Unbound is here to turn the tables.
